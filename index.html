How to play Colamone

(1) Colamone is kinda like chess. Only some loose similarities. (2) Each piece can move 1 tile in the direction of any of it's dots. (3) If a piece gets to the other side, you get how many ever points it's worth. (4) If you get 8 points, you win. (5) When a piece gets to the other side, you can no longer move it and the enemy cannot take it. (6) When a player can't make any more moves, whoever has the highest score wins. (7) if the current position on the board appeared 3 times then the result is a draw.
